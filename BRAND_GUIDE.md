# SafePay Brand Guide

## Brand position
SafePay is a premium wearable payments control app. The brand should feel secure, modern, fast, and highly controlled.

## Logo idea
- A glowing circular mark inspired by a smart ring
- Center dot signals secure tokenization and tap activation
- Wordmark uses bold, confident typography

## Palette
- Background: `#050B18`
- Deep surface: `#111F39`
- Primary electric blue: `#2F80FF`
- Success green: `#31D0AA`
- Warning amber: `#F4B860`
- Danger red: `#FF5C7A`
- Main text: `#F4F8FF`
- Secondary text: `#9BB0CF`

## Typography
- Hero: 30
- Title: 24
- Subtitle: 18
- Body: 15
- Caption: 13
- Micro: 11

## Interaction direction
- Smooth page transitions
- Quick card carousel motion
- Subtle confirmation glow on approved payments
- Slide-up card detail / bottom sheet
- Gentle switch/toggle motion
- Soft alert emphasis for security-critical states

## UI states
- Approved = success green
- Pending / verify = amber warning
- Frozen / blocked = soft red
- Connected / ready = green chip
- Offline / needs action = amber or red chip

## Core tone
- Professional
- Reassuring
- Direct
- Premium
- Minimal
